Vehicle Code - VEH

DIVISION 12. EQUIPMENT OF VEHICLES [24000 - 28244]

  ( Division 12 enacted by Stats. 1959, Ch. 3. )
  

CHAPTER 5. Other Equipment [27000 - 28160]

  ( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1959, Ch. 3. )
  

ARTICLE 1. Horns, Sirens and Amplification Devices [27000 - 27007]
  ( Heading of Article 1 amended by Stats. 1978, Ch. 1306. )

  
27001.  

(a) The driver of a motor vehicle when reasonably necessary to insure safe operation shall give audible warning with his horn.

(b) The horn shall not otherwise be used, except as a theft alarm system which operates as specified in Article 13 (commencing with Section 28085) of this chapter.

(Amended by Stats. 1977, Ch. 993.)
